How does this actually work?

Your password is hashed with SHA-1 entirely inside your browser — the real password never leaves your device. Only the first 5 characters of that hash are sent to the Have I Been Pwned API. It responds with every breached hash sharing that prefix (thousands of them), and the matching is finished locally. This is called k-anonymity: the server never receives enough information to know which password you checked.
